DermaIQ: AI-Powered Skin Health Assistant

A computer vision–driven web platform to detect 10 facial skin conditions and provide personalized guidance through an intelligent AI assistant.

Understanding the Problem

Skin health is a daily concern, but access to accurate and accessible diagnosis is limited. Many users struggle to understand their skin issues, often relying on vague searches or unverified advice.

DermaIQ tackles this by offering a reliable, private and easy-to-use diagnostic flow powered by deep learning and intelligent assistance.

How DermaIQ Works

A guided diagnostic flow built with computer vision and generative AI. From scanning to consultation — it all happens in one elegant experience.

Guided Skin Scan

Users upload 3 facial images (left, front, right). A custom-trained YOLO model isolates the facial region and prepares it for accurate analysis.

Multi-Class Segmentation

Each region of the face is segmented into 10 disease classes including acne, blackheads, and pigmentation. Results are visually overlaid for user clarity.

Results + Assistant Advice

Scan results are processed and explained by “Alex,” an AI assistant powered by LLMs using a RAG-based pipeline. Alex suggests treatments, tips, and answers follow-up queries.

Interactive Dashboard

DermaIQ includes a user-friendly dashboard where all past scans are securely stored and accessible. Users can review previous results, track changes over time, and monitor progress — enabling better skincare decisions with historical context.

Innovation & Learnings

Building DermaIQ challenged us to strike a delicate balance between medical accuracy and user-friendly design. From fine-tuning computer vision models for facial segmentation and ROI isolation, to crafting an intuitive and empathetic AI assistant — every stage demanded technical precision and human-centered thinking.

The process deepened our understanding of developing multi-modal AI systems and designing robust data pipelines capable of managing complex, real-world tasks smoothly and reliably.

Most importantly, we learned the power of AI that not only informs — but connects. DermaIQ goes beyond diagnostics; it offers a thoughtful, supportive experience that helps users better understand and care for their skin.
